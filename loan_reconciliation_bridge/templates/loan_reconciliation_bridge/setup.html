How It Works

Configure the staging account that receives loan payments during bank reconciliation.

Workflow:
  1. Bank Transaction - Customer payment received
  2. Reconciliation - Assign to Loan Payments Received
  3. Loan Info - System shows customer loan details
  4. Approval - Process loan payment allocation
Benefits:
  • Clean separation of received vs allocated
  • Loan customer information display
  • Approval workflow before allocation
  • Complete audit trail
